About General Assembly


UNGA (DISEC) was established by the UN to serve as a platform for representatives from all the member nations to consider the general principles of co-operation in the maintenance of international peace and security. It takes the measures necessary for the prevention or the reduction of international hostilities on matters that are not under discussion by the Security Council. BITSMUN continues to remain faithful to its original values - professionalism, commitment to excellence and high levels of debating.
